    About

    MREIF

    The Mortgage Refinance Initiative Fund — a federal mortgage financing programme. Copyright held by Ministry of Finance Incorporated (MOFI); the fund is managed by ARM (Asset & Resource Management Holding Company), with offices in Lagos and Abuja.

    Mission: bridge Nigeria's housing gap by providing accessible financing options and supporting the development of affordable housing across the nation.

    9.75%Fixed rate p.a.
    20 yrsMax loan term
    10%Min. equity

    For homebuyers: create a free account → select a property → get pre-approved → secure your mortgage.

    For developers: can apply to list eligible projects — minimum 5 hectares of titled land, ₦500m net asset value, and 3+ completed projects delivered in the last 5 years.

    Visit MREIF's official site → Start a MREIF application →

    housedata.ng is not affiliated with MREIF. This is a general summary for orientation only — always confirm current terms and eligibility directly with MREIF.
